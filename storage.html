QR Code System
Feature 01

Unique Unit Identity

Every blood bag receives a QR code at collection. The code encodes blood type, donor ID, collection date, center location, and batch number — all linked to the central platform.

Feature 02

Scan-at-Every-Step

QR codes are scanned at collection, during storage checks, at dispatch, and at hospital receipt. Each scan logs a timestamped entry in the immutable audit trail.

Feature 03

Expiry Alerts

Platform automatically flags units approaching their 42-day expiry window. Redistribution orders are triggered to minimize wastage across the network.

Feature 04

Instant Recall

In the event of a donor health update or contamination flag, any affected unit can be instantly identified and recalled across all locations using the QR link.
