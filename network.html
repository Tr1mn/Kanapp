Network Pillars

Cross-Border Agreements

Bilateral and multilateral agreements govern cross-border blood transport, removing customs barriers for certified GBS shipments.

Rare Type Sourcing

A global registry of rare and ultra-rare blood types — Bombay (Oh), Rh-null, and others — enables rapid international sourcing when regional supply is zero.

WHO Compliance

Every partner nation operates under harmonized WHO blood safety standards. Annual audits and real-time dashboards ensure continuous adherence.

WHO Framework

Global Blood Safety Strategy 2020–2025

All GBS operations are fully aligned with the WHO Global Strategy for Blood Safety and Availability, encompassing voluntary non-remunerated donation, universal screening, and quality systems.

Crisis Response

Pandemic & Disaster Protocols

GBS maintains pre-authorized emergency stockpiles in six global hubs. During declared crises, international redistribution is activated within 6 hours of WHO alert issuance.
