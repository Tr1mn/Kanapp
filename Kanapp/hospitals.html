Hospital Features

Live Inventory View

See blood availability across all networked banks within your region. Filterable by type, Rh, quantity, and expiry window.

Emergency Protocol

One-click emergency request triggers multi-bank sourcing simultaneously. Response SLA under 90 minutes in urban zones.

Full Audit Trail

Every unit received comes with a complete chain-of-custody log — donor ID, collection date, storage history, and transport records.
